My Story

There once was a minor magic minion named Krog. Krog wasn't
very popular, his skin was weird and had no beard. He always wanted
to join the other minor magic minions in fun games. But due to being teased
he lost hope in joining. That was until he started wearing his new hood! It was
special and new, made by his mom for his birthday! Made from the softest materials
around. It was as warm as a winter coat, as soft as a blanket, and as cooler than anything
else around! As Krog wore the hood, he found himself being talked to more! With people wanting to
sit with him at lunch! Finally he felt like he could join everyone!
